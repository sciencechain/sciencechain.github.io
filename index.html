About Sciencechain

Originally a 'demo,' LAB was originally released by Coinworksdotinfo as "Coinlabcoin" and then "Coinworkscoin." It is a CPU based 9-round (3-random) 6-hash cryptographic blockchain token based on Bitcoin and Quark.

LAB was fairly launched, has zero premine, and a rapid distribution to 1,000,000 tokens with ongoing proof of work at one token per block forever.

LAB was abandoned when Coinworks went offline but in the spirit of open source and to maintain this technically sound and enticingly valuable token alive, it has been rebranded and the client updated to Core level code. Sciencechain is 100% compatible with the original LAB chain and client, no swapouts required and keys will import directly.
